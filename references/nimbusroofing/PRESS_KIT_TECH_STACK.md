## AI-Powered Features

### **1. Autonomous Storm Content Generation**

**How It Works:**
1. Weather monitoring service checks NWS API every 5 minutes
2. When severe weather alert detected in service area, AI triggers content generation
3. RAG system retrieves relevant proprietary data (insurance claims, building codes, past projects)
4. Gemini AI generates 1,000-1,500 word SEO-optimized blog article
5. AI creates featured image matching article topic
6. Content auto-publishes to website with geo-targeted keywords
7. Owner receives notification of published content

**Business Impact:**
- Captures search traffic within minutes of storm events
- Targets homeowners searching "roof damage McKinney" immediately after hail
- Average $4,200+ supplement value per insurance claim
