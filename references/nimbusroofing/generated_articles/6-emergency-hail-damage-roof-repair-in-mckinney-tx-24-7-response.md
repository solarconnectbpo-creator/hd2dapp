## Identifying Hail Damage: What McKinney TX Homeowners Should Look For
After a severe storm, many **McKinney TX homeowners** may not realize they have sustained damage. Hail damage is often subtle and can be missed by the untrained eye. However, even small impacts can compromise the integrity of your shingles, leading to premature failure. If you suspect your roof has been hit, call us for a **free roof inspection**.

### Common Signs of Hail Damage
*   **Missing Granules:** Look for bald spots on asphalt shingles where the protective granules have been knocked off. You may find these granules in your gutters.
*   **Dents and Dings:** Check metal components like gutters, downspouts, and vents for visible dents. If metal is dented, your shingles are likely damaged too.
*   **Bruising on Shingles:** On asphalt shingles, hail can cause a "bruise" or soft spot where the matting underneath has been fractured. This is a common sign of structural damage.
*   **Damage to Other Property:** Check decks, fences, and siding. If these items show signs of impact, your roof almost certainly does as well.

### The Importance of a Professional Inspection
